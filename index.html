Map Animation

My map animation is centered on the capital of Oahu, HI, Honolulu. The first marker is placed near my home in Makiki and the directions will have the endpoint of Kaimanas Beach. The different components (CSS, JS, HTML) have been seperated into seperate files within the MapAnimation folder & the map style has been changed to satellite for a different aesthetic. As well as the button has also been styled in the seperate CSS in order to make it stand out. With each marker move the viewer can see turn by turn the route to the beach. The duration of the trip has been sped up to 10x the normal time it would take for the sake of viewing pleasure and time.
